Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 6, 2026

At BelmontCragin.com, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our visitors and community members. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, use, and secure your personal information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you interact with our website, such as when you:

  • Subscribe to our community newsletter (e.g., your email address).
  • Submit a business or worker listing to our Resource Directory (e.g., your name, business name, address, phone number, and description).
  • Send us inquiries or feedback through email or contact forms.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for purposes designed to support our community portal, including to:

  • Maintain, display, and verify directory listings in our resource hub.
  • Deliver community updates, announcements, and newsletters to subscribers.
  • Respond to your feedback, questions, or support requests.
  • Monitor and analyze website usage trends to improve user experience.

3. Data Protection and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures—including SSL encryption and secure hosting headers—to safeguard your information. However, please be aware that no transmission of data over the internet can be guaranteed as 100% secure.

4. Cookies and Analytical Tracking

BelmontCragin.com may use cookies (small text files placed on your device) to enhance site performance, remember user preferences, and compile aggregate data about site traffic. You can adjust your browser settings to disable cookies if you prefer.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. This does not include trusted hosting partners who assist in operating our website, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you, request corrections to incorrect details, or request that we delete your newsletter subscription or directory listing. Please contact us at the address below to make these requests.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us at: admin@belmontcragin.com.
